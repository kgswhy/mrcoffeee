Our Story

Our Journey

The Beginning

Founded in 2010 by Susanto Liman, MR.COFFEE started as a small coffee shop with a big dream. Our founder's passion for coffee and dedication to quality quickly made us a favorite among coffee enthusiasts.

Today, we've grown into a beloved brand while maintaining our commitment to crafting the perfect cup of coffee for every customer.
